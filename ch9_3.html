Perspectives for the future

This project has now concluded. While the artistic outcomes have been presented and the accompanying reflections are essentially complete, lingering questions remain, and new opportunities have emerged that I see as worth exploring. To continue unraveling some of these outward threads in a future project, I believe the ideas outlined in the later stages of the project suggest a promising direction for further research. This involves creatively investigating and developing technological tools in conjunction with AI to discover new forms of artistic expression, emphasizing the need to reshape ideas about music, art, the artist, materials, and many other aspects that are rapidly evolving. I believe this is critically important, as we find ourselves in a pivotal period of human history regarding the development of artificial intelligence, making this endeavor feel less like merely "working on what you love" and more like a mission that must be pursued.
